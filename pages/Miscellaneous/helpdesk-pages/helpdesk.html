How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password. Follow the link in the email to create a new password.

When is the deadline for course registration?

Course registration deadlines vary by semester. For the current semester, registration closes on September 15th. Late registration is available until September 20th with a late fee.

How do I access my official transcripts?

Official transcripts can be requested through the Records Office in your student portal. There is a $10 fee for each official transcript. Processing typically takes 3-5 business days.
