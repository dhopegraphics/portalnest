APPLICATION FORM FOR INSTITUTION CREATION

Before application for registration:

a. School must be registered at the Registrar
General’s Department.
b. School must be registered at the Local
Authority where it is located. After a
successful application for registration, the
applicant will be notified.
c. school must have document provided by
National Schools Inspectorate Authority
(NaSIA)
d. The National Schools Inspectorate Authority
(NaSIA) formerly the National Inspectorate
Board (NIB) is an autonomous agency under
the Ministry of Education (MoE) established
by the Education Act, (2008) Act 778 as
amended by the Education Regulatory Bodies
Act 2020, (Act 1023).

The Act 1023 mandates the Authority to
“develop, publish, promote, and enforce
highest quality standards and guidelines for
quality education in public and private pre-
tertiary education institutions” in Ghana.
The Authority is also responsible for regulating
all of Ghana’s public and private pre-
tertiary schools.
