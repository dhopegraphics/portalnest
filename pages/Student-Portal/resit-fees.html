Resit Examination Information
Registration Open

Resit examinations are available for students who have failed a course and wish to retake the examination without repeating the entire course. Each resit examination requires a separate fee payment.

Please note that you can only register for resit examinations for courses you have previously taken and failed with a grade of D or F. The maximum number of resit examinations you can register for in a semester is 3.
